About

A Practical, Business-First Approach to AI

I founded Kimrey Consulting to help businesses cut through the noise around AI and technology and focus on what actually moves the needle: clearer processes, better decisions, and teams that are equipped to keep up.

My background spans healthcare operations, insurance, and business strategy — giving me a grounded perspective on how AI intersects with real-world constraints like compliance requirements, workforce readiness, and budget realities.

I study business at the University of Michigan and hold a Michigan insurance producer license. I work with clients in healthcare, small business, insurance, and the public sector — wherever the opportunity to do things smarter exists.

My Consulting Philosophy

The best technology strategy is one your team will actually use. I don’t recommend tools for the sake of it — I focus on helping you understand where AI creates genuine leverage in your specific context, and what it takes to get there without unnecessary disruption.

I work best with founders, operators, and department leaders who are serious about improving their organizations and want a thinking partner who will be honest with them.

Process

How We Work Together

Every engagement is different, but the process is consistent: understand first, then recommend, then execute together.

1

Discovery

We start with a conversation — no pitch, no agenda. I want to understand your business, your team, and where you’re feeling friction.

  • Introductory call
  • Current-state review
  • Goal alignment
2

Assessment

I take what I’ve learned and dig deeper — mapping workflows, identifying opportunities, and sizing the realistic effort required.

  • Workflow documentation
  • Opportunity identification
  • Risk and feasibility review
3

Strategy

I deliver a clear, prioritized plan with specific recommendations — what to do, in what order, and why, with honest trade-offs surfaced.

  • Prioritized recommendations
  • Implementation sequencing
  • Success metrics
4

Execution Support

Strategy without follow-through doesn’t stick. I remain available to support implementation, answer questions, and adjust as things unfold.

  • Hands-on support
  • Progress check-ins
  • Iteration and refinement

Flexible Engagement Models
